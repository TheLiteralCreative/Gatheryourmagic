From Commission to Live System in 8 Weeks

The MVP is scoped to deliver exactly what was commissioned — no more, no less. The timeline is structured so that the most critical piece (the inventory system) is completed first, with the customer-facing features built on top of it.

Week 1
Infrastructure setup, hosting, and domain configuration
Weeks 2–3
Database foundation — all 30,000+ MTG cards loaded and searchable
Weeks 4–5
User accounts, store account setup, and secure login
Week 6
Card search interface — fast, fuzzy, autocomplete
Weeks 7–8
Store inventory management, Kiosk Mode, and Public Storefront
End of Wk 8
Beta launch with your store as the first live client
