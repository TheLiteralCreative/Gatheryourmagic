Customers Find What They Need — Without Asking Every Time

The Kiosk Mode transforms any tablet, laptop, or monitor in your store into a customer-facing search terminal. It is locked to a read-only view of your inventory — customers can search and browse, but they cannot change anything.

What a customer sees

  • Search bar with instant autocomplete
  • Card image, condition, and your price
  • Physical location in the store
  • How many copies you have in stock

Staff Impact

Instead of interrupting an employee to ask "do you have a Rhystic Study?", the customer checks the kiosk, finds it in Binder 4, and brings it to the register.

Your staff spends less time searching and more time selling.
