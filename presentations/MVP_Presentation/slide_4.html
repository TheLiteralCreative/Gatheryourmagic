Every Card, Catalogued and Findable in Seconds

The inventory system is the foundation. Every card in your collection is entered once and stored in a structured database that can be searched instantly by any employee or customer.

Getting the data in

Cards can be added one at a time through the search interface, or imported in bulk via a spreadsheet (CSV) upload — ideal for getting your existing inventory digitized quickly.

What gets tracked for each card:

Identity

Card name, set, and edition

Condition

Mint, Near Mint, Lightly Played, Moderately Played, Heavily Played, Damaged

Finish

Foil or non-foil status

Pricing

Your asking price

Physical Location

e.g., "Showcase A", "Binder 3", "Backroom Box 12"

Market Value

Current market value (updated nightly from market data)
