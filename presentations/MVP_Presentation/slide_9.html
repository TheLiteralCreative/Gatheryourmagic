Three Things to Kick Off the Build

The project is ready to begin. To move from planning to development, three things are needed from the client side:

1. A card inventory export or list

In any format (spreadsheet, handwritten list, photos of binders). This becomes the first data import and the first test of the system.

2. Store details

Name, address, and preferred public URL slug (e.g., /stores/mycomicshop).

3. A point of contact

One person on the store's team who will be the primary tester and feedback provider during the 8-week build.

Everything else is handled on the development side.
