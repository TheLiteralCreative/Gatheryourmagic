GatherYourMagic Turns Your Inventory Into a Searchable Database

GatherYourMagic is a purpose-built platform for exactly this problem. The MVP delivers three core capabilities, designed specifically around how a local game store operates.

01

The Inventory System

A complete, searchable digital catalogue of your entire MTG collection, with condition, location, and pricing for every card.

02

The In-Store Kiosk

A locked-down search terminal customers can use on a tablet or screen in your store to find cards themselves.

03

The Public Storefront

A public web page at gatheryourmagic.com/stores/[yourstore] where customers can browse your inventory from home before they visit.
