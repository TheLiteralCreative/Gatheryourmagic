# GatherYourMagic.com
## Executive Summary

*The world's first AI-powered TCG coaching and collection platform.*

---

## The Opportunity

The global Trading Card Game market is valued at **$13.28 billion in 2025** and is projected to reach **$24.36 billion by 2030** — a near doubling in five years. *Magic: The Gathering* alone has been in continuous print since 1993, with over **30,000 unique cards**, millions of active players across 70+ countries, and a passionate community that spends generously on both physical cards and the digital tools to manage them.

Despite this scale, the digital platform ecosystem serving these players is fragmented, outdated, and fundamentally passive. Existing tools — Moxfield, EDHREC, MTGGoldfish — are sophisticated databases. What they are not is **intelligent**. They tell players what other people are playing. They do not tell players how to play better.

**That gap is the market. GatherYourMagic is the solution.**

---

## The Platform

GatherYourMagic.com is a unified, AI-powered platform that serves as a collector's archive, a strategist's workbench, and a coach's playbook — all in one place. It is built first for *Magic: The Gathering* and architected to expand across the full TCG ecosystem.

The platform delivers four core experiences no single competitor currently offers together:

| Feature | What It Does | Why It Wins |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **The Library** | Full collection management with card scanning, variant tracking, and real-time portfolio valuation | Replaces spreadsheets and fragmented apps with a single source of truth |
| **The Coach** | AI-powered deck analysis that reviews a user's decklist against their goals and collection, then gives specific, actionable advice | Moves beyond statistical aggregation into genuine strategic mentorship |
| **The Oracle** | A real-time rules referee that answers complex gameplay questions in plain English, grounded in official rules | Eliminates mid-game disputes and reduces the barrier to entry for new players |
| **The Strategist** | Metagame-aware recommendations showing how a deck performs against the current competitive field | Gives competitive players the edge they are actively seeking and paying for |

## The Market Segment

GatherYourMagic targets three distinct and overlapping user segments, each with strong monetization potential:

**The Casual Collector** (largest segment) — Players who own hundreds to thousands of cards, play primarily Commander (the game's most popular format), and want to organize their collection, build thematic decks, and understand the game more deeply. They are underserved by tools built for competitive players and highly receptive to a friendly, coaching-oriented experience.

**The Competitive Grinder** (highest LTV) — Tournament players who track the metagame obsessively, optimize every card slot, and spend significantly on new releases and singles. They demand precision data, matchup analysis, and format-specific optimization tools.

**The New Player** (fastest growing) — MTG is actively expanding its player base through digital products (MTG Arena) and mainstream IP crossovers (Assassin's Creed, Final Fantasy, Marvel). New players are entering the hobby without a mentor and are the most likely to pay for a guided, educational experience.

---

## The Competitive Moat

GatherYourMagic's advantage is not any single feature — it is the **integration** of all of them, powered by an AI layer that understands the game at a structural level. The platform's Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) architecture grounds every recommendation in official card data and rules, preventing the inaccuracies that plague general-purpose AI tools when applied to MTG. Competitors cannot replicate this overnight; it requires deep domain knowledge, purpose-built data pipelines, and a game-first design philosophy.

Additionally, the platform is designed for **multi-game expansion** from day one. Pokémon TCG, Yu-Gi-Oh!, Disney Lorcana, and One Piece TCG are all in scope, each with established APIs and growing player bases. Every new game added multiplies the addressable market without requiring a platform rebuild.

## Business Model

GatherYourMagic operates on a **freemium subscription model** with four tiers:

- **Free** — Core search and basic collection tracking. Drives user acquisition.
- **Enthusiast ($4.99/mo)** — Unlimited collection, portfolio valuation, full deck builder.
- **Champion ($9.99/mo)** — Full AI Coach, Oracle referee, matchup simulator, trade tools.
- **Store/LGS ($29.99/mo)** — Multi-user management, event tools, inventory pricing for Local Game Stores.

The Local Game Store tier is a particularly high-value channel: there are approximately **3,000+ game stores** in North America alone, each representing a recurring revenue relationship and a physical marketing presence within the community.

The TCG market is large, growing, and deeply loyal. Its players are passionate, technically engaged, and accustomed to spending money on the tools and cards that improve their game. The platform they deserve — one that truly understands the game and coaches them through it — does not yet exist.

**GatherYourMagic is going to build it.**
