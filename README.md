# GatherYourMagic.com

> *Your collection. Your coach. Your single source of truth.*

GatherYourMagic is a next-generation TCG platform for collectors, coaches, and champions. It combines comprehensive card library management with an AI-powered deck-building coach, a real-time rules referee, and multi-game support — starting with **Magic: The Gathering** as the primary focus.

## Project Vision

GatherYourMagic is more than a card database. It is:

- **The Archivist** — A searchable, filterable database for your entire physical and digital card collection across MTG, Pokémon, Yu-Gi-Oh!, and more.
- **The Coach** — An AI-powered deck advisor that understands your collection, your playstyle, and your format, and gives you specific, actionable advice.
- **The Oracle** — A real-time rules referee that answers complex rules questions in plain English, grounded in the official Comprehensive Rules.
- **The Strategist** — A metagame-aware tool that helps you understand how your deck performs against the current competitive field.
